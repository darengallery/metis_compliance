About the company

Metis is a relatively new company being incorporated in 2018. However, its principal Matthew Shaw has over 25 years experience.

The ethos of the company is to be client focused, we value every client whether large or small, whether we undertake a single training course for you or offer on-going monthly services.

We understand that time is sometimes of the essence, especially, when hot file reviews need to be undertaken.

Metis aims to offer a cost effective solution to compliance for all accountancy practices irrespective of their size or nature.

We can offer the following services:

  • Cold File Reviews
  • Hot File Reviews
  • Annual Compliance Reviews (Whole Practice / Client Monies / AML / DPB)
  • Help plan for a regulatory compliance visit and addressing the points raised by them
  • Training
  • Technical advice and ad-hoc consultancy

We have also secured for our clients a discount on the costs of software provided by AMLCC.

About the team

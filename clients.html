Clients

Metis’ clients include one of the UK's Accountancy Regulatory Bodies, firms of Chartered and Certified Accountants ranging in size from a 20 partner firm to sole practitioners with no staff. Many of our clients are firms of registered auditors and some of our clients are boutique practices whose clients include charities, academies and listed companies, this means that Metis is experienced at dealing with technical issues covering a variety of topics at the highest level.

We also provide technical advice for accountancy practices registered with the Institute of Financial Accountants (IFA).

Our clients are not limited to accountancy practices. We also provide advice to accountants working in industry and offer anti-money laundering training, consultancy and help with compliance to Estate Agents , Art Market Participators (AMPS) and other organisations that work in the AML regulated sector.
